About

Annette

Rooted in Halifax and guided by a deep love for movement and stillness, Annette has been teaching yoga for over a decade. Her practice is a blend of breath, intention, and community — meeting every student exactly where they are.

Whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or deepening an existing practice, Annette creates a space that feels both grounding and expansive. She teaches privately and brings yoga into workplaces across Halifax to help teams find focus, ease, and connection.
